## Embedding Powerpoints in Jekyll Posts
* Create a Powerpoint or Google Slide Presentation
* Save to Microsoft One Drive
* File -> Share -> Embed 
* Copy -> Paste in date-your-blog-post-here.md
* $ $ $  
     
That's all.
